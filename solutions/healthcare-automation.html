The Challenge: Balancing Innovation with Compliance

Healthcare providers in the EU face immense pressure to innovate while navigating a stringent regulatory environment. The administrative burden on staff is enormous, and the risk of data breaches with sensitive patient information is a constant concern. Adopting new technology is essential, but it must be done with an uncompromising focus on security and GDPR compliance.

Our Solution: Security-First Automation

We provide tailored automation solutions architected specifically for the EU healthcare landscape, ensuring you can innovate with confidence.

GDPR-Compliant Workflows

We automate administrative tasks like patient onboarding, appointment reminders, and billing using self-hosted n8n instances. This ensures all patient data remains within your control, guaranteeing data sovereignty and compliance.

Secure AI for Clinical Support

Our AI agents, using secure GraphRAG technology, can query internal medical records and knowledge bases to provide clinical staff with instant, contextual information without exposing sensitive data to external models.

Enhanced Data Security

Every solution we build incorporates end-to-end encryption, robust access controls, and detailed audit logs. We help you reduce manual data handling, which is a primary source of data breaches, by automating information flows securely.
